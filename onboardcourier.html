Onboard Courier Custom Critical "hand-carry" Service

A vetted and experienced representative can personally pick up your custom/time critical package/letter and head to the closest airport.

Once they get to the airport, they will board the earliest available flight to the desitnation country. They will always keep your consignement on their person and take it onboard the plane as a 'carry on', and ensure its safe delivery to it's destination.

Once the representative lands at the destination country, they will immediately head to the delivery address. Your consignment and the representative's movements will be tracked from pick up to delivery.

Case Study Business Contract from Manhattan, New York to King's Cross, London

Below is an example of a client needing an important business contract delivered to King's Cross, London.

  1. 07.00am (EST) - The contract is picked up from the client's office in Manhattan, New York.
  2. Ensure collection is signed for, checked and photographed.
  3. Status update.
  4. Head to JFK.
  5. 08.15am (EST) - Board the earliest available flight at JFK.
  6. Status update.
  7. 08.10pm (GMT) - Land at LHR, London.
  8. Clear customs with carry on.
  9. Status update.
  10. 09.20am (GMT) - Head to the delivery address immediately.
  11. Hand deliver the consignment and have proof of delivery signed.
  12. Final status update.
