Onboard Courier Custom Critical "hand-carry" Service

A vetted and experienced representative can personally pick up your custom/time critical package/letter and head to the closest airport.

Once they get to the airport, they will board the earliest available flight to the desitnation country. They will always keep your consignement on their person and take it onboard the plane as a 'carry on', and ensure its safe delivery to it's destination.

Once the representative lands at the destination country, they will immediately head to the delivery address. Your consignment and the representative's movements will be tracked from pick up to delivery.
